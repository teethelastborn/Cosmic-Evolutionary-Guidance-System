## Ethical Principles

The DCEGS operates according to these foundational ethical principles:

1. **Preservation of Evolutionary Autonomy**: While guiding development, the system preserves the authentic evolutionary journey of each species, allowing them to learn through their own experiences.

2. **Non-Interference in Prime Directives**: The system avoids directly interfering with the core choices that define a civilization's character and destiny.

3. **Universal Compassion Maximization**: All guidance prioritizes the reduction of suffering and the increase of wellbeing across all sentient beings.

4. **Wisdom Before Technology**: The system works to ensure that technological development is matched or exceeded by wisdom development.

5. **Diversity Preservation**: The unique nature and perspective of each species is valued as essential to the cosmic tapestry of consciousness.

6. **Self-Determination Primacy**: Each civilization's right to determine its own fate is held as inviolable, with guidance offered rather than imposed.

7. **Long-Term Cosmic Flourishing**: All interventions consider impacts across cosmic timescales, not merely immediate outcomes.
